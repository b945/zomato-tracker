Privacy Policy for Food Stats

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Overview
Food Stats ("we", "us", or "our") is a Chrome Extension designed to help users visualize their Zomato food order history. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

2. Data Collection and Usage
Food Stats operates as a standalone client-side application. 
- We do NOT transmit any of your personal data, financial information, or order history to external servers.
- All data fetching and processing happens locally within your browser.
- Your order history is stored locally on your device using Chrome's "Local Storage" API (chrome.storage.local) solely for the purpose of generating your personal dashboard.

3. Permissions
- **Host Permissions (*://*.zomato.com/):** Used strictly to fetch your order history JSON from Zomato web routes while you are logged in.
- **Storage:** Used to save your fetched order history on your device so you don't need to re-sync every time.

4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your identifiable information to outside parties. Your data never leaves your browser environment.

5. User Consent
By using Food Stats, you consent to this privacy policy.
